ABOUT US

ABOUT US – THE ARTS 2024

Located in the heart of Agadir historic district, The Ard exudes the charm of classic European cafés, blending tradition with modern sophistication. Set in a beautifully restored heritage building, our interior features grand arches, intricate woodwork, and elegant chandeliers that create a warm and inviting atmosphere.


Our menu is a celebration of Agadir's finest local produce, offering a delightful mix of classic and contemporary flavors. From succulent seafood to exquisite meats and creative vegetarian dishes, every meal at The Ard is crafted with passion and precision.


Complement your dining experience with our artisanal cocktails and fine wines, while our resident pianist provides the perfect soundtrack for an unforgettable evening.


At The Ard, every visit is a step back in time and a journey into elegance. Join us for a romantic dinner, a special celebration, or simply to enjoy life's finer moments.


Discover The Ard – where history, elegance, and exceptional taste meet.

Meet The Ard Team: Passionate, Dedicated, and Ready to Serve

At The Ard, our team is the heart and soul of everything we do. From our talented chefs crafting exquisite dishes to our friendly staff ensuring a seamless dining experience, every member brings their unique passion and dedication to the table.


Together, we are committed to making every visit to The Ard unforgettable. We take pride in our work and in creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where guests can relax and enjoy the finest in food and hospitality.


Join us at The Ard and become part of our story.
